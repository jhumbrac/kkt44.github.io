travel

There are 3 intrnational airports with international that serve Biala Podlaska, the closest town to the wedding venue. These are:

  1. Okecie (Chopin) Airport in Warsaw,
  2. Modlin Airport (Near Warsaw)
  3. Lublin Airport

Okecie Airport in Warsaw - it is one of the biggest airports in Poland with good connections to various destinations. It is relatively central and has great and frequent, in comparison to other airports, transport links to central Warsaw (bus – approx. £1, taxi rides - approx. £10, overground train - approx. £1). Car rental is also available. More info can be found here www.lotnisko-chopina.pl

Modlin Airport – this airport is located approx. 30km outside Warsaw. It serves mainly Ryanair flights. There is a reasonable bus service to central Warsaw that takes approx. 40min – 1 hr and it costs approx. £2 per journey. www.modlinairport.pl

Lublin Airport - this airport is located approx. 30 km away from Lublin. It has a limited number of flights and transport links are limited to bus to Lublin and car rentals. Renting a car would be highly recommended if you choose to fly to this airport. www.airport.lublin.pl

Accommodation on night before the wedding:

As flying on the wedding day is not an option you may want to stay in Warsaw or Biala Podlaska the night before the wedding.

Warsaw – there are plenty of hotels that will fit all budgets. Booking.com will have many options to choose from. We recommend staying somewhere central, somewhere close to the main train station (Warszawa Centralna). We can also recommend a few hotels and B&Bs if you get in touch.

If you intend to spend a day or two in Warsaw Warsaw Tour may be a good website for touristic information.

Biala Podlaska- we recommend Hotel Skala. Could you please let us know if you would like to stay here as we can arrange for a group booking and hopefully get a better price.
