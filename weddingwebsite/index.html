travel

Okecie Airport in Warsaw - the biggest airports in Poland.It has great and frequent transport links to central Warsaw (bus – approx. £1, taxi rides - approx. £10, overground train - approx. £1). Car rental is also available. More info can be found here www.lotnisko-chopina.pl

Modlin Airport –located approx. 30km outside Warsaw. It serves mainly Ryanair flights. There is a reasonable bus service to central Warsaw that takes approx. 40min – 1 hr and it costs approx. £2 per journey. www.modlinairport.pl

Lublin Airport - located approx. 30 km away from Lublin. It has a limited number of flights and transport links are limited to bus to Lublin and car rentals. Renting a car would be highly recommended. www.airport.lublin.pl
