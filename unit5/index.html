UNIT 5: - Build your own website: Part 1

For the reminder of the course I will be building a wedding website for my friends who are getting married in July 2015.

Step 1: Define your Audience/ Tone

Who will visit your site? Potential employers?

New clients or customers?The website will be visited by family and friends invited to the wedding.

What can make you and/or your product stand out against your competitors?

There are no competitors as such - very specific audience with no need for checking out other wedding websites.

How would you speak to your clientele if you were communicating in person? How could you translate that style and tone digitally?

The purpose of the website is to provide useful information to those attending the wedding, so I will keep it short and to the point. The layout of the website, the ease of navigation and clear structure will translate the style and tonne digitally.

What colours represent you or your product?

The wedding pallet will include simple colours - white (R= 255, G=255, B=255), black (R=0, G=0, B=0) and red (R=160, G=7, B=33) and different opacities of these colours.

What images illustrate your message?

There will be a photo of the couple getting married on the homepage to let visitors know that they are visiting the right website. There will also be images of a wedding venue and a map of how to get there. Other images to be determined during next few weeks.
